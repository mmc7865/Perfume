    • about Brand

    At Adil Qadri Perfumes, the art of perfumery is crafted into a science of everlasting memories through their fragrances. Adil Qadri stands strong as a corporate entity with a vast portfolio of over 300 of the finest and most captivating fragrances. The brand has a strong retail presence with over 240+ exclusive retail outlets across the GCC and the world. Adil Qadri also has a presence on the international front; currently exporting to 45+ countries across the globe and with an exclusive presence through select 30 global Duty-Free locations and International Airlines.

    In India, Adil Qadri Perfumes is available at 3000 Points Of Sale across a mix of channels which comprises of Modern Trade, E-commerce, General Trade, Multi Brand Outlets, and Owned Retail. Adil Qadri Perfumes are now available at select stores in Shoppers Stop, DaburNewU, Parcos, Olfactive, Jade Blue, Wellness Forever amongst others. In e-commerce, Adil Qadri is present across approximately 40 sites where Adil Qadri is one of the biggest single-brand perfumery house
